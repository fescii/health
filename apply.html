Healthcare Services

Join our elite team of healthcare professionals delivering concierge medicine excellence.

Key Responsibilities

  • Provide comprehensive primary and preventive care to concierge medicine clients
  • Conduct executive physicals, wellness assessments, and chronic disease management
  • Deliver 24/7 medical support via phone, email, and virtual consultations
  • Perform in-home visits and mobile medical services across Nairobi and surrounding areas
  • Coordinate with specialists and provide medical advocacy for clients
  • Maintain detailed electronic medical records and personalized health plans
  • Support emergency medical evacuation and critical care coordination

Required Qualifications

  • Medical Degree (MBChB, MD, or equivalent) from recognized institution
  • Valid medical license to practice in Kenya
  • Board certification in relevant specialty (preferred)
  • Minimum 3-5 years of clinical experience
  • Strong communication and interpersonal skills
  • Commitment to patient-centered, personalized care
  • Technology proficiency for telemedicine and digital health platforms

Preferred Specialties

  • Family Medicine & General Practice
  • Internal Medicine
  • Emergency Medicine
  • Preventive Medicine
  • Geriatrics
  • Cardiology, Endocrinology, or other internal medicine subspecialties
  • Travel Medicine certification is a plus

Additional Requirements

  • Current CPR and ACLS certification
  • Malpractice insurance or willingness to obtain
  • Flexibility for irregular hours including nights, weekends, and emergency calls
  • Valid driver's license and reliable transportation for home visits
  • Fluency in English and Swahili preferred
  • Experience with concierge medicine or direct primary care models preferred
