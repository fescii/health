Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Diasync International

What is concierge medicine?

Concierge medicine is a membership-based healthcare model where patients pay a monthly or annual fee in exchange for personalized care, enhanced access to their physician, and a closer doctor-patient relationship with 24/7 access and longer visits.

Do I still need insurance?

Yes. Concierge membership provides enhanced access and care but does not replace traditional health insurance. Insurance is still required for major medical services, laboratory tests, medications, imaging, and hospitalizations.

Is emergency evacuation covered?

Ground and air evacuation services are available only with an active subscription. A 5-day waiting period applies after your subscription is activated. We provide ICU-level care with evacuation coverage across Kenya and to South Africa for certain plans.

What if I'm traveling abroad?

You'll have 24/7 remote access to your physician while traveling. We coordinate care and provide medical support wherever you are, including assistance with healthcare needs and emergency planning while abroad.

Can I manage my care online?

Yes. Through our platform, you can book appointments, join video consultations, chat with providers, view lab results, and access your complete medical records securely online.

Who benefits most from concierge care?

This model is ideal for busy professionals, families, seniors, and individuals with complex or chronic health needs who value personalized attention, convenience, and comprehensive healthcare coordination.
